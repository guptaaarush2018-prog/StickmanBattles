# STICKMAN BATTLES — CANON

## CORE TRUTHS (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

1. The Creator and True Form are the SAME ENTITY
   - The Creator is a constructed shell (armor/system layer)
   - The True Form is what exists inside
   - During the ending, the True Form escapes the Creator

2. True Form is the FINAL fight of the main story
   - Nothing comes after it in the main arc
   - It represents the system’s deepest layer being revealed

3. Fallen God is POST-STORY CONTENT (Godfall Arc)
   - NOT stronger than True Form
   - NOT the final boss of the main story
   - Role: evaluator, not surpasser
   - It tests the player AFTER everything is resolved

4. Story progression is LINEAR and LOCKED
   - No branching into separate categories (e.g., "Multiverse Mode")
   - Multiverse content is PART of the story, not separate

5. Player Power Curve Must Be Fair
   - No normal enemy can one-shot the player
   - Boss difficulty must feel intentional, not impossible

---

## POWER HIERARCHY

1. True Form (Final System State)
2. Creator (Shell / Interface Layer)
3. Rift Entity (Fragment Bearer)
4. Architects
5. High-tier enemies
6. Standard enemies

Fallen God:
- Exists OUTSIDE this hierarchy
- Acts as observer/tester, not a power escalation

---

## THEMATIC RULES

- Power comes from adaptation, not raw scaling
- Every fight should feel learnable
- The story is about:
  - control vs freedom
  - systems vs individuals
  - carrying responsibility

---

## HARD RULES (DO NOT BREAK)

- True Form must ALWAYS be final main boss
- Fallen God must NEVER replace True Form
- Creator and True Form must NEVER be split into separate beings
- Multiverse is NOT a menu — it is part of progression
